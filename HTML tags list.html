How to write HTML entities

They all start with &-l-t-;(less than)and end with&-g-t-;(greater than) with entity name in the middle.

Always start with & and end with ;

Example:

& lt;!DOCTYPE & gt;
        & lt;a & gt ;
        & lt;abbr & gt ;
        & lt;acronym & gt ;
        & lt;address & gt ;
        & lt;applet & gt ;
